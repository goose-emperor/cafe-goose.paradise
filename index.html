About Us


The Goose Paradise Cafe was founded in 1867 by Felix Goulon Macker in

France, Paris, Saint Goose Street.

At first the business went into decline, there was a thought about closing.

But then his faithful friend came to Felix’s aid, and his business was going well.

With his help, the design and prices of the cafe were changed and customers began to visit. Years passed, the cafe changed, Felix died.

The business passed into the hands of his son, Mark. Under his management, the cafe again fell into disrepair.

Unfortunately, there was no friend like Felix. The cafe was still losing money. Owners changed until Felix Emperor I Goose eventually took over.

He opened cafe branches all over the world, from Argentina to Japan. Its most popular cafe is located at Austria, Vienna, Josefstädter Strasse 25/1.

Under his ownership, the cafe flourishes and still blooms and smells.
